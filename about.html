About the Club

Richmond Athletic Football Club was founded in 2014 by Connor Ford and Chuckie Humphries. Two JMU undergrads who had the vision to create an elite adult league team in Richmond during the summer. The team consists of former high school players from around the greater Richmond area. Richmond Athletic's goal is to become the premier adult league team in the CVSA and Richmond area.

Originally T-shirts and Cups during the Summer 2012 season, Ford led the team to third place overall. In a tough semi-final match against Richmond City FC, T-shirts and Cups were eliminated.

During the Summer 2013 season, the team was reformed under the name Randomnation, adding Garrett Braun, Tyler Harper, and Chuckie Humphries to the roster. Randomnation finished second overall before losing in the semi-finals to Screaming Eagles FC.
